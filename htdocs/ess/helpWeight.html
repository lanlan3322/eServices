Split% / Weight

This field let's you specify the percent of the total report* that you want charged to this purpose. Enter the percent you want charged to this purpose and select "Split among purposes" when you enter each expense.

Percents for all customers do not have tosum to100%.  The system actually sums the total of numbers in the "Split % / Weight" fields and compares each individual field against this total to determine the true percent to be applied to a purpose.  To equally split expenses among purposes, a simple "1" in each field will suffice.

Purposes that have a blank "Split % / Weight" field do not participate when expenses are split.

*actually, this is the total expenses not charged to specific purposes
